Samoel Henrique



Samoel was the most common old man you would ever see in your life, at the same time he was a great water bottle flipper.


History


Born at 1950 in England, he was forced to work since 12 to help his family survive, at 1969 he discovered his most brillant talent, at 1000 bottle flip he was able to success in 998, at the time this ability was not considered important which make people laught at him, and ask him why to waste so much time just flipping bottles.


At 1999 he was able to do something never seen before, he managed to successfully flip 6 water bottles at the same time! At the time no one could stand against him when the subject is bottle flipping, he was exceptional.


But time has come, his wife Josefa Alencar, had been captured by bad guys, they required Samoel to pay € 1.000.000 if he wanted to see her again, how Samoel would get € 1.000.000? He had a genius idea, he would get to the location and impress every bad guy with his unappreciated talent! This would make they see how talented Samoel is and let his wife go.


At the location, Samoel called every bad guy to see his ability, they became so impressed, everything was going as planend, but the big bad guy, didn't happy with such performance offered a deal, if he was able to win against his 9 y.o. son at bottle flip, his wife would be free, otherwise, they both would meet the death, without options Samoel accepted.


His son appeared from the shadows, he was wearing student clothes, and by looking at him, anyone would say he is the smartest boy in the school, without saying nothing he throw a water bottle in a table 10 meters await from him, Samoel was impressed but he needed to stay calm!


The fight began, the boy did ballet moves while throwing bottles, every throw was perfect, it was really something out of the world, as Samoel saw it, he had little hope of getting out of this with life... to beat this boy he had to try the most dangerous bottle flip of all time, The deadly bloody bottle flip of death.


He took the knife of one of the bad guys and was ready for the moviment, he threw the knife up, he threw the water bottle up and he used all his strength in it's old body to make a backflip, the time seemed to slow down, everyone with their mouth wide open to this scene, Samoel, saw all his life throught his eyes, all the bad times, all the good times, and all the bottles he never flipped... When he thought about all bottles he didn't flip, he knew, it's not his time yet, he had to flip a lot more of bottles, and then he found him self in the ground.


The bottle has perfectly above his head, he did make it! But had a miscalculation in the knife weight, instead of reaching the top of the bottle, it's reachs the side, cutting the side of the bottle in a half, and reaching Samoel's head, everything goes black...
